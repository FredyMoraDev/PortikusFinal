INFLORESCENCE

Radio Inflorescence is a bi-monthly program, which comprises a series of commissioned podcasts, material from various sound archives and shows developed in collaboration with the Frankfurt-based radios as part of Ximena Garrido-Lecca’s exhibition Inflorescence at Portikus. The program can be both experienced on site as well as online.
